Find The Family Game!

Directions

Try and return the Vaquita Porpoise back to her family by clicking around the screen. If something comes up saying Bottom Of The Ocean that means that you are as far away as possible from the Vaquita. If it says Deep Sea that means that you are very far away but is closer that Bottom Of The Ocean. Middle Ocean means that you are half way there. Almost At The Surface means almost there and Land Ho means that you basicly couldn't be closer.
